"Seoul: Where It All Began"

From the vibrant streets of Myeongdong to the tranquil beauty of Hanok villages, Seoul is a city of contrasts.

Aspect Details
Landmark Namsan Tower (N Seoul Tower) – Offering panoramic views of Seoul.
Culture & History Bukchon Hanok Village and Gyeongbokgung Palace – Reflecting Seoul's rich cultural and historical heritage.
Food Kimchi, Bibimbap, Tteokbokki – Iconic dishes showcasing Korean flavors.
Experience Wearing a hanbok while exploring historic sites.
